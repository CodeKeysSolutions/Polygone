About:

To move a full stack, make sure "AMOUNT" is empty or greater than the full stack.
Use your mouse wheel to set the amount you want to move. You can do this during and before dragging.
To USE an item, drag the item to the "USE" hand.

Keybinds:

  • Shift + Click: Sends half of the stack to the other inventory.
  • Ctrl + Click: Sends the amount entered in "AMOUNT" to the other inventory.
  • Left Click: Drag Item
  • Middle Click: Fast-Use Item
  • C: Clears "AMOUNT".
  • Ctrl + C: Copy item data

Stores:

Set the amount you want to buy in "AMOUNT" and then drag the item to your inventory.

Crafting:

It's always useful to remember the required materials.
When crafting, you must enter the exact amount you can craft. If you want to craft more than is in the crafting bench, it will craft the maximum the bench has.

Item Lifetime:

Almost all items will decay (including weapons).
The bar under each item will slowly shrink as it decays and will eventually fade to a reddish color. Once an item is fully destroyed, the entire slot will have a red background.
